Company background

MB DEWATERING is a 100% black-owned company, registered in South Africa providing dewatering services to construction, mining and industrial clients. The owner, Mr. Mohapi, has over ten years hands-on experience in water management.

Mission

To provide high quality services, working hand-in-hand with our clients to meet excellent results according to clients' needs and ensure continuous improvement of machinery and pumps, exceeding our client’s expectations.

Vision

To be the most trusted service among competitors in the sector, achieving excellent outcomes through a skilled and experienced team.

Our Services

Dewatering — Solid-liquid separation

Rapid and safe removal of water and solids from construction sites, quarries and mining operations using self-priming pumps.

Dam cleaning — Environmental dam clean up

We clean silted dams, lined dam silt removal and environmental cleanup to restore capacity.

De-sludging — Removal of dry sludge

Removal and off-site disposal of dry sludge and semi-sediments, including desludging ponds and tanks.

Industrial cleaning

Sump cuts, canal silt removal, tank and digester cleaning for industrial sites.

Sand & slurry pumping

Pumping and handling sand, slurry and abrasive materials using robust submersible pumps.

Emergency Response

Rapid deployment for flood response and emergency water removal work.
