About MB Dewatering

MB DEWATERING is a 100% black-owned company, registered in South Africa with the mission to provide dewatering services for the best outcome of all types of construction, health and safety projects.

The company was founded by Mr. Mohapi, who has over ten years of hands-on experience in the industry. His vision is to provide high-quality services that exceed client expectations while ensuring environmental safety.

We believe in working hand-in-hand with our clients to achieve excellent results according to their specific needs, with continuous improvement of our machinery and processes.

Our Services

Our Services

We cover all aspects of the life cycle of a dewatering project

Dewatering

Solid-liquid separation services for construction sites, quarries, and mining operations. We use high-quality pumps and equipment to ensure efficient water removal.

Dam Cleaning

Environmental dam clean-up services to maintain water quality and prevent contamination. We specialize in removing sediments and pollutants from water bodies.

De-sludging

Removal of dry sludge and semi-sediment from sites. Our specialized equipment ensures safe and efficient sludge removal and disposal.

Industrial Cleaning

Comprehensive industrial cleaning services including sump clean-out, canal silt removal, and lined dam silt removal for various industrial applications.
